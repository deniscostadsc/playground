# Bella, the saucy puppy

Miss Nana received a quite saucy puppy named Bella. The puppy was causing a lot
of mess and leaving her owner with frizzy hair. To educate her, Nana adopted a
reward approach: whenever Bella behaves by making mess no more than 3 times per
week, she gets her favorite cookie.

This is working well as Bella is quite intelligent and is realizing that when
she doesn't make many mischiefs during the week, she earns her little cookie.
However, Bella is very crafty and sometimes ends up exceeding the maximum limit
of mess set by her tutor, thus not getting her reward.

In this exercise, given the number of messes Bella made during the week, you
should inform whether she will get the cookie or not.

## Input

You will receive an integer $N (0 \leq N \leq 100)$, representing the number of
times Bella made mess during the week.

## Output

You should print the response 'will get the cookie' (without quotes) if Bella
meets Nana's requirements to earn the cookie. Otherwise, print 'will not get the
cookie' (without quotes).
